2. The Rise and Fall of X86

2. X86的兴衰

X86's Remarkable Run (1985-Present)

X86的卓越历程（1985年至今）

X86 grew from a tiny slice of the market in the 1980s to dominate not only PC but also data center markets. Its volume advantage in PCs allowed it to completely dominate minicomputer and RISC-based Unix systems with superior economics and better price performance, and ecosystem leverage. Ironically, X86 markets started to get a little sloppy early last decade, but then it bounced back during COVID.

X86从20世纪80年代市场的一小部分，发展到主导PC和数据中心市场。其在PC领域的销量优势使其能够凭借卓越的经济性、更好的性价比和生态系统优势，完全主导了当时的迷你计算机和基于RISC的Unix系统。 讽刺的是，X86市场在上个十年初期开始出现一些混乱，但在新冠疫情期间又反弹了。

Intel's Dominance: Volume, Value, Velocity

英特尔的主导地位：销量、价值、速度

Intel's PC chip revenue and volume exploded over 15 years thanks to the Wintel monopoly. Intel became the largest semiconductor manufacturer in the world, surpassing all pure-play silicon companies and IBM. As Intel took over the PC space, other enterprises went upmarket. PC volumes conferred value to X86, allowing Intel to wipe out HP, IBM, Sun, and others in the enterprise, holding nearly all of the data center market (well over 90%) by the 2000s.

得益于Wintel垄断，英特尔的PC芯片收入和销量在15年内爆炸式增长。英特尔成为全球最大的半导体制造商，超越了所有纯芯片公司和IBM。 随着英特尔占据PC市场，其他企业转向高端市场。PC销量为X86带来了价值，使英特尔在企业市场中淘汰了惠普、IBM、Sun等公司，到2000年代，几乎占据了所有数据中心市场（远超90%）。

Intel created volume, driving down costs. Microsoft created value. Together, they removed impediments to adoption, increasing velocity.

英特尔创造了销量，从而降低了成本。微软创造了价值。两者共同消除了采用障碍，提高了速度。

The catalyst was the shift to microprocessor-based systems.

这让人想起1990年的IBM，当时它创下了创纪录的收入，利润相对于1989年增长了62%。这种“死猫跳”预示着IBM在1991-1993年期间的痛苦时期，伴随着巨大的亏损和内部混乱。 IBM曾是世界顶级的半导体制造商，却被迫支付GlobalFoundries 15亿美元，以摆脱其微电子业务。其催化剂是向基于微处理器的系统转变。

To keep foundry viable, Intel needs wafer scale volume. This means leaning into ARM and RISC-V for both its own internal designs and to attract external customers. For its advanced designs, Intel must go to TSMC to shore up its PC and server business, compete with AMD, get its volume up, and get to market faster.

为了保持代工厂的生存能力，英特尔需要晶圆规模的产量。这意味着要依靠ARM和RISC-V，既用于其内部设计，也用于吸引外部客户。 对于其先进设计，英特尔必须转向台积电，以巩固其PC和服务器业务，与AMD竞争，提高产量，并更快地进入市场。
